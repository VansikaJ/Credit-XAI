Overview

Why CreditLens?

Traditional credit scoring treats applicants as black boxes — approved or rejected with no explanation. CreditLens changes that by combining high-accuracy ML with real-time explainability.

🎯

Accurate Predictions

CatBoost classifier trained with Optuna Bayesian optimization achieves 94% accuracy and 0.91+ AUC — robust even on imbalanced financial datasets.

🔍

Transparent Reasoning

Every decision comes with LIME-based local explanations and SHAP global feature importance — no more opaque black-box decisions.

🧪

What-If Analysis

Users can adjust inputs like income, employment years, or education to instantly see how changes affect their approval probability.

💡

Credit Coaching

Personalized, actionable recommendations delivered by the Credit Coaching Engine — guiding users from Bronze to Platinum health status.

📊

Rich Visualizations

Approval probability gauges, LIME bar charts, SHAP plots, and feature contribution visuals — all rendered with Plotly inside Streamlit.

🛡️

Regulatory Ready

SHAP and LIME outputs create auditable, traceable decision logs — compliant with financial audit and fairness requirements.

Explainability

Two Layers of Transparency

CreditLens combines global and local explainability methods so both applicants and credit officers can understand every decision.

Global Explainability

SHAP Analysis

Shows which features matter most across the entire model — giving credit officers and data scientists an aggregate picture of model behaviour.

  • Age and employment years have the strongest influence
  • Annual income consistently a top-3 predictor
  • Education level shows moderate but meaningful impact
  • Marital status and income type contribute directionally
Local Explainability

LIME Explanations

Explains exactly why this specific applicant received their result — surfacing which factors pushed them toward approval or rejection.

  • Per-applicant feature contribution bars
  • Positive and negative factor separation
  • Directly actionable rejection reasoning
  • Integrates seamlessly into the What-If simulator
